Darktide Crit Blessings Analysis

An examination of weapons with interesting blessings that benefit from critical hits, based on thoughts from Alexander Graham.

Weapons with Crit-Synergy Blessings

The following weapons possess blessings that trigger powerful effects upon scoring a critical hit, turning a lucky shot into a decisive moment.

Combat Axes

  • Decapitator: Gain a bonus to Finesse (including critical damage) on one-shot kills.

Tactical Axes

  • Slaughter Spree: Gain +100% critical chance on your next melee attack after a weakspot critical hit kill.

Chainswords, Relic Blades & Cleavers

  • Devastating Strike: Gain +80% cleave for 5 seconds on a critical hit.
  • Heatsink (Relic Blades): Critical hit kills reduce heat.

Quickdraw Stub Revolvers

  • Hand Cannon: Gain +60% rending on a critical hit.

Autoguns & Heavy Stubbers

  • Deadly Accurate: Gain +100% critical weakspot damage.

Shotguns & Ripper Guns

  • Flechette: Inflict bleed stacks on a critical hit (+6 for shotguns, not specified for Ripper).
  • Manstopper: Increased cleave and +25% ranged stagger on critical hit.

Bolt Pistols

  • Deadly Accurate: Gain +100% critical weakspot damage.

Combat Blades

  • Flesh-tearer: Apply +8 Bleed stacks on a critical hit.
  • Precognition: Gain +60% Finesse damage on successful dodge.

Heavy Melee Weapons

(Heavy Swords, Eviscerators, Bully Clubs, Latrine Shovels, Power Mauls, Slab Shields)

  • Perfect Strike: Critical Hits ignore enemy hit mass from armour, and grant +10% melee critical damage.

Dueling Swords & Blaze Force Swords

  • Precognition: Gain +60% Finesse damage on successful dodge.

Power Falchions

  • Heat Sink: Weakspot kills and Critical Hit kills reduce heat by a percentage over 3s.

Laspistol & Grenade Gauntlet

  • Infernus (Laspistol): Apply Burn stacks on critical hits.
  • Reassuringly Accurate: Gain a percentage of toughness back on a critical hit kill.

"A lot of these are new! I'm pretty sure Infernus used to be the las pistol's only crit blessing. I must have missed the patch notes when this happened"

Plasma Guns

  • Gets Hot!: Critical chance scales up to +10% based on heat level, and adds +10% ranged critical hit damage.
  • Focused Cooling: +60% heat generation on critical hit. (Note: Graham suspects this might be a typo)

Twin-linked Heavy Stubbers

  • Charmed Reload: Load 5 bullets from reserve on a critical hit.

Psyker Force Swords (Blaze)

  • Blazing Spirit: Enemies gain 4 stacks of Soul Blaze on a critical hit, to a maximum of 12 stacks.
  • Warp Slice (Greatsword): Guaranteed Activated Critical Strike on a 15s cooldown.

Psyker Force Staves

  • Surge (Voidblast, Electrokinetic, Voidstrike): Fire 2 shots on a primary critical hit. Also increases ranged critical chance.
  • Blazing Spirit (Voidblast, Voidstrike): Enemies gain 4 stacks of Soul Blaze on a critical hit.

Flamers

  • Everlasting Flame: Critical hits expend ammo from your reserve rather than the magazine.

Honourable Mentions: Crit Chance Boosters

While the weapons above benefit *from* crits, the blessings on the following weapons are notable for their ability to *increase* the chance of landing them in the first place, setting the stage for devastating combos.

Universal Blessings: Shred (Bonus crit chance on chained hits) is found on Combat Axes, Tactical Axes, Chainswords, Devil's Claw Swords, Dueling Swords, Heavy Eviscerators, and Blaze Force Swords/Greatswords. Surgical is available for Quickdraw Stub Revolvers, Vigilant Autoguns, Bolt Pistols, Boltguns, Helbore Lasguns, Kickbacks, and Heavy Stubbers.

Chainswords / Chainaxes / Blaze Force Swords: Bloodthirsty grants +100% critical chance on the next melee attack after a special attack kill.

Shotguns (Exterminator, Combat, Riot Shield, Double-barreled): Scattershot gives +12% critical chance for each enemy hit by the previous attack, stacking up to 5 times.

Quickdraw Stub Revolvers / Vigilant Autoguns: Crucian Roulette offers +6% (or 0.6% for Autoguns) critical chance for each empty round in the magazine.

Revolvers / Bolt Pistols: Point Blank provides +20% crit chance for 2.5 seconds after a melee kill.

Vigilant Autoguns / Heavy Stubbers / Lasguns (Infantry, Recon): Headhunter grants +20% critical chance on a weakspot hit until the next critical hit, stacking 5 times.

Shredder Autopistols / Boltguns / Ripper Guns / Heavy Stubbers: Cavalcade increases crit chance for every 10% of the magazine spent during continuous fire.

Combat Blades / Dueling Swords / Blaze Force Swords: Riposte grants +20% critical chance on a successful dodge. Blades and Bully Clubs also get Smackdown for +20% crit chance on staggering an enemy with a special action.

Heavy Swords: Chained Deathblow gives +20% critical chance on a weakspot kill.

Laspistol: Features Concentrated Fire (+crit chance on chained weakspot hits) and Desperado (+crit chance on successful dodge).

Latrine Shovels / Cleavers: Shovels have Sucker Punch (+crit chance on special action hit). Cleavers have Bash (+crit chance on pushing enemies).

Grenade Gauntlets: Pulverise grants +25% critical chance on a melee kill.

Plasma Guns: Power Blast gives between 5% and 25% critical chance based on charge level when firing.

Psyker Force Staves: Warp Nexus provides critical chance based on current Peril. Certain staves also get Surge for bonus crit chance and extra shots.

All information based on community findings. Data sourced from the Darktide Wiki.
